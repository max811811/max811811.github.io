About Me

Hello! My name is Max McNally and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest started back in 2000 as one of the first players of Halo. My first real exposure to coding was during my teens with Runescape auto clicker mods. Turns out all those hours wasted on PC games led me here!

Fast-forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of working at a large developer, a start-up, a huge corporation, and some companies in the middle. My main focus these days is building accessible products and digital experiences.

I also am working on an API that provides software trend data.

Here are a few technologies I’ve been working with recently:

  • Languages: JavaScript, Python, HTML, CSS
  • Frameworks/Libraries: React, Node.js, Express.js, Django, Mongoose
  • Databases: MongoDB, SQL, PostgreSQL
  • Cloud Platforms: AWS, Azure, Google
  • Artificial Intelligence : Textract, Deep Learning(Tensor Flow)
